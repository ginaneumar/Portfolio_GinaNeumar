Overview

Background

The task of the project is to design a Website or App that people can travel safe in Germany while Corona. It is the first project where I should do a design run through. Here I learned about the context of use, interviews, personas, wireframe, prototypes and usability.

Goal

The goal was to create a website that allows users to travel during Corona and get all the necessary information from our website.

Role

UX Designer

Target Group/Persona

1 / 2

Laura is a student who likes to travel to explore new places and pursue her hobby of photography. While Corona was she actually not travel at all, because her grandmother is pre-sick and thus does not want to face any risk. Since she has no plans to travel anytime soon, a website is good but she doesn't depend on it.

2 / 2

Lukas loves traveling regularly with his family. During Corona he willingly renounced, but he missed it very simply to take the time of a few days. A website would be very helpful for him to find out exactly where it is safe to go.

Prototype

High-fidelity Prototype

For our final prototype, we decided on a color scheme of light blue, black and gray. The map of Germany has an orange to red color to show how many corona infections there are currently in these areas.

On the start page you can directly see the map of Germany and the filter for it. You can filter by activities, new infections, price class, federal state, distance and measures. If you then apply the filter, you can see the various accommodations, etc. that offer all this and you can see the marker for it on the map.

Of course it is possible to click on a marker to get more detailed information.

There are also pages for the travel checklist and current Corona news.

You can either check off the travel checklist directly on the website or download and print it.

In our project, it was also important to include accessibility, and that you can control these issues in the settings.

Reflection

What did I learn

It was the first time we got a real job for a design. Thereby I gained an insight into what the process of a designer is like. Here I could also work with Balsamiq and Axure for the first time. With this project I also realized how much fun I have with this kind of projects and its process.
